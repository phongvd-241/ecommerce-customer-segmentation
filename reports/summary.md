## Exploratory Data Analysis Insights

Initial EDA was performed on the cleaned transaction dataset.

Key areas analyzed:

1. Overall revenue, customers, orders, products, and countries.
2. Monthly revenue trends.
3. Top countries by revenue.
4. Top products by quantity and revenue.
5. Top customers by revenue.
6. Customer spending distribution.
7. Order value distribution.
8. Orders by day of week and hour.

Main observations:

- Revenue appears to be concentrated among top countries and top customers.
- Customer spending is highly skewed, suggesting the need for customer segmentation.
- Product-level analysis shows differences between best-selling products and highest-revenue products.
- Time-based patterns can support promotion timing and operational planning.
